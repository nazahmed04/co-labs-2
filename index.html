Charities making a change

Lifeshare

Life share is the oldest charity dedicated to the Homeless in Manchester. Their objectives include; identifying street homelessness and offering assistance in obtaining emergency accommodation, signposting other relevant agencies and information for support, alleviating aspects of poverty and hardship through redistribution of resources. By promoting health and harm reduction, they assist those in need of access Primary Health Care.

Barnabus

Barnabas is a Christian Homeless Charity in Manchester for over 30 years. Their drop-in centre offers food, showers, clothing, access to medical care and activities. Barnabas also host a support office in which they offer accommodation referrals, advice resettlement, and employment. In 2005 Barnabas received Queen Elizabeth II's "Unsung Heroes" award for Voluntary Service.
